**Dada Devs**

*Building Africa's next generation of Bitcoin open-source contributors.*

Dada Devs develops technical talent through structured learning pathways, collaborative engineering, and open-source contribution.

We believe Bitcoin is built by contributors. Through collaborative engineering, technical mentorship, and real-world open-source development, we prepare developers to build, review, test, document, and contribute to the software and public infrastructure that powers Bitcoin.

## Why We Exist

Bitcoin is global infrastructure, yet women—particularly African women—remain significantly underrepresented among its open-source contributors.

Dada Devs exists to help close that gap by creating clear pathways into meaningful technical contribution, ensuring more African women participate in building the future of Bitcoin through engineering, collaboration, and public open-source development.

## What We Do

Dada Devs develops technical talent through structured learning pathways, collaborative engineering, and open-source contribution.

Rather than treating Bitcoin education as the end goal, we focus on preparing contributors who can participate meaningfully in real-world open-source development. Our developers gain hands-on experience studying Bitcoin systems, collaborating with maintainers, contributing to public repositories, reviewing code, writing documentation, testing software, and building Bitcoin applications and infrastructure.

Alongside our technical programs, we are building a collaborative technical hub in Nairobi where contributors, mentors, and visiting developers can work together, exchange ideas, and strengthen Africa's presence in Bitcoin open source.

## Our Approach

- *Contribution before credentials.* We believe meaningful contribution is the best way to learn Bitcoin engineering.
- *Learn by building.* Contributors develop their skills through practical
- *Mentorship from practitioners.* Contributors learn alongside developers actively building Bitcoin and Lightning software.
- *Community as infrastructure.* Collaboration, code review, mentorship, and peer learning are essential to developing long-term contributors.
- *Engineering with impact.* We encourage work that strengthens Bitcoin's public infrastructure and benefits the wider ecosystem.

## Areas of Focus

## Get Involved

Dada Devs grows through collaboration with the global Bitcoin open-source community.

We welcome:

- *Maintainers* interested in supporting new contributors.
- *Reviewers and mentors* willing to share their expertise through code review and technical guidance.
- *Organizations supporting Bitcoin open-source development* that want to strengthen the contributor pipeline.
- *Developers, designers, researchers, and technical writers* interested in building the future of Bitcoin alongside us.
